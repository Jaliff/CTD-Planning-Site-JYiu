Making my subpages

-After the wireframe making, we proceeded to create a template on dreamweaver.

For the general look and feel, we tried to follow the objectives closely to create and easily navigable pages with as little additional elements as possible, compensating aesthetic clutter for ease of use and minimalism. the image below shows the general layout of our DW template prior to the addition of navigational elements.

-there are 3 editable regions in this template and each are not designated for a specific purpose. they are intended to be large spaces that are flexible and changes depending on the subpage.

-The basic idea of this subpage follows the same format as the homepage, with three editable regions that will be used for images or text depending on the subpage
